Welcome to TrueStory

TrueStory is a revolutionary social media platform dedicated to transforming the way news is shared and consumed.

Our mission is to create a more trustworthy and transparent news environment, combining the speed and community engagement of social media with the accuracy and reliability of traditional news sources.

The Power of Community

At TrueStory, we believe in the power of community input.

Our platform enables users to share news quickly and freely, without the restrictions often imposed by traditional news agencies.
This helps us quickly deliver news and updates to our users, keeping everyone in the loop and well-informed.

The Truth-O-Meter

Central to our platform is the Truth-O-Meter, an advanced AI model that classifies posts based on their authenticity.

By analyzing various factors, the Truth-O-Meter provides an initial assessment of each post's truthfulness, helping users navigate the vast amount of information available online.

Trusted Users

In addition to the AI-driven Truth-O-Meter, TrueStory relies on trusted users to verify or disprove news posts.

These users play a crucial role in maintaining the integrity of the platform, ensuring that the information shared is accurate and reliable.

Trusted News Source

TrueStory aims to be THE reliable news resource.

By leveraging the expertise of our trusted users and the advanced capabilities of AI, we strive to provide our users with a platform where they can confidently engage with the news.

Truth-O-Meter

  • Definitely False: Absolutely false.
  • Probably False: Likely false but not confirmed.
  • Not Sure: Uncertain, further verification needed.
  • Probably True: Likely true but not confirmed.
  • Definitely True: Absolutely true.

Trusted Users

  • Verified: Confirmed to be true by a trusted user.
  • Disproven: Confirmed to be false by a trusted user.
